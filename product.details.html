About Tanqueray London Dry Gin

Tanqueray London Dry Gin is a classic, premium London Dry Gin first created in 1830 by Charles Tanqueray.
It's renowned for its bold, juniper-forward character and is distilled four times for exceptional clarity and smoothness.

Best enjoyed: In a classic Gin & Tonic with lime, or a dry Martini.
